About Us

Get to know who we are, where we come from, and what drives our innovation in access control.

Experienced Team

Our team brings decades of experience in the access control industry. We combine technical knowledge, innovative thinking, and a customer-first mindset to deliver high-quality solutions. Our engineers, developers, and support staff work together to provide reliable and secure systems.

Locally Manufactured

Proudly South African, Saflec Systems designs and manufactures all access control products locally. This ensures high-quality standards, rapid support, and the flexibility to customize solutions that meet the unique needs of African markets and beyond

Our Story

Saflec Systems is a leader in access-controlled security, offering SACS an offline system where door controllers make local access decisions. This setup ensures reliability, faster response times, and continued operation even during PC or network failures, making it ideal for wide-area company networks.

SACS also allows optional online control for advanced tasks or future upgrades. Designed in South Africa, our products reflect decades of expertise. Saflec Systems is part of the Saflec Group, alongside Saflec, a specialist in metal detection technology.
